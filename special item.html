Macchiato

A macchiato is sometimes known as an espresso macchiato or a caffè macchiato. It's an espresso with a splash of milk, to put it simply. Macchiato is an Italian word that means "marked, soiled, or speckled." This description relates to the milk’s mark when you add it to the dark, rich espresso. The amount of milk in the coffee is just enough to brighten the color and puncture the smooth, black surface.
